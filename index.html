Dothraki

In George R.R. Martin's famous novel, A Song of Ice and Fire, and HBO's award winning TV Series, The Game of Thrones, Dothraki is a language spoken by the Dothraki community living in the vast central plains of ESSOS. The Dothraki's are known for their bond with the horses. Their bond with their horses is such that it is said that they born, fight and die on the saddle.
